How Your Carbon Footprint is Measured:

  • Electricity: Each kWh of electricity typically produces 0.4 kg CO₂e.
  • Fuel: Burning 1 liter of gasoline produces about 2.3 kg CO₂e.
  • Flights: Each flight taken emits roughly 250 kg CO₂e.
  • Food: The carbon footprint of food varies but typically averages 3 kg CO₂e per kg of food.
  • Waste: Generating 1 kg of waste is estimated to emit 2.5 kg CO₂e.
